Worlds happiness, GDP & Population

This visualisation explores which Countries in 2020, have the most life satisfaction and how does this correlate to the nation's GDP per Capita and the median age of the people of the nation

The choropleth map above shows the life satisfaction of the countries in the world in year 2020.

we can see that the Canada ( Top Left corner ), Iceland, Norway, Sweden, Finland, Ireland, Switzerland, Germany, Denmark, Netherlands, Austria ( Middle Top ), Australia and New Zealand ( Bottom Right corner ) are in blue which shows that their people very satisfies their life.

Whereas, Papua New Guinea, Fiji, Timor-Leste, Brunei ( Bottom Right corner ), North Korea ( Top Right corner ), Bhutan, Oman, Qatar, Sudan, South Sudan, Central African Republic, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Angola, Guinea-Bissau ( Middle ), Guyana, SurinameTrinidad and Tobago, Haiti, Cuba, Bahamas, Belize ( Left ), and Greenland ( Top Left corner ) are in blood red color which shows that their people are not satisfy their living now, which we can say that they are living in sadness.
